About

Yes, you pronounce my name the same way you would for “cayenne pepper.”

My design journey started from seeing cool video edits of movies and shows I like to it now being my chosen career. Being a storyteller at heart, I love experimenting with video and photo manipulation to create memorable and unique designs that enlist a deep sense of emotion and connection.
